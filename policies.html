Payment

A non-refundable deposit of £15 is required to secure a booking, which is deducted from the total price. Booking confirmation emails will contain payment details.

Lateness

Grace periods are given, but late fees apply afterwards:
10 mins = £10
15 mins = £15
20 mins = £20
Appointments may be cancelled if lateness interferes with other appointments.

Cancellations

Cancelling an appointment less than 48 hours in advance incurs a 50% charge. Rescheduling less than 48 hours before an appointment requires another £15 deposit.

Emergency Appointments

For emergency or out-of-hours appointments, direct message (DM) to enquire. Out-of-hours appointments are for times not found on the calendar and require a £10 fee, to be paid with the deposit.
