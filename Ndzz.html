About Nailed By Nadzz

Leeds' Premier Mid-Century Salon

Established in 2021, Nailed By Nadzz brings the timeless elegance of mid-century design to the beauty industry. Our salon is designed to be a cozy retreat where you can relax and be pampered.

Our team of highly skilled professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional service using only the highest quality products. We believe that beauty treatments should be both luxurious and accessible.

Located in the heart of Leeds, our salon is easily accessible by public transport and has parking available nearby.
